An open source LawRefBook App.

Features:
- Open source: Lightweight, clean and secure.
- App Style: Follows Material Design guidelines.
- Search: Title, content highlight match.
- Hierarchical directory: Locate content quickly.
- Offline support: No network hassles.
- Diverse sharing: text, pictures, there is always a way you like
